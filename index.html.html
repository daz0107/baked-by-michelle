Welcome to Baked by. Michelle:

Where Every Bite is a Celebration!

At Baked by. Michelle, we take pride in crafting delightful cakes and savory dishes right from our cozy kitchen. As a small, home-based business, we pour our heart and soul into every creation, ensuring that each bite is made with dedication and care. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a quiet meal at home, our homemade treats are designed to bring joy and satisfaction.

Our cakes are a true labor of passion. From classic chocolate and vanilla to custom designs, we create cakes that not only taste amazing but also look great. Each cake is baked fresh, using quality ingredients with no preservatives to make sure it is a moist, flavorful experience that will make any celebration memorable, all at affordable prices that won’t hurt your pockets.

But we don’t just stop at sweet treats. At Baked by .Michelle, we also offer a selection of savory dishes that are perfect for any meal of the day. Our savory offerings include everything from tasty puffs and pizzas to flavorful pastries and more. Each dish is crafted with the same attention to detail and passion that goes into our cakes, providing a satisfying and delicious addition to your dining table, all while keeping prices affordable.

As a small business, we value every customer and aim to create a personal experience with each order. Whether you’re ordering for a small event or just for a special treat at home, we’re committed to delivering quality and taste that goes beyond your expectations without breaking the bank.

Thank you for choosing Baked by. Michelle. We look forward to being a part of your special moments and everyday joys. Feel free to reach out and contact us with any questions or to place an order, we’re here to make your taste dreams come true!
